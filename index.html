Real-Time Screen Mirroring
MirrorU is a screen mirroring app for both iOS and Android devices. It allows screen mirroring with audio and offers an ultimate mirroring experience. With MirrorU, users can screen mirror iPhone/iPad and Android devices to PC via USB or WiFi and cast phone to TV without lag.
Control Device from PC
Users can connect their iPhone/iPad and Android devices to PC with or without cable, and control their device from PC by using a mouse and typing with a keyboard. This screen mirroring app also allows users to play mobile games on PC without emulators. With its pre-set virtual keys, users can show their godlike skills effortlessly.
